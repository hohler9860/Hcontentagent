## FILMING NOTES

- **Camera Setup:** iPhone on tripod, vertical (9:16), eye-level. Green screen. This can absolutely be a single continuous take with green screen images swapped in post -- fastest to produce of all 5 scripts.
- **Location:** Home office with green screen. Minimalist.
- **Pacing:** Ultra-fast. This is the 18-second energy of "A big price tag doesn't mean rare" (14,700 plays) stretched to 25 seconds with three reveals. Each watch gets exactly one punchy statement + price. No dwelling. No over-explaining. One point per watch, move on.
- **Music/Sound:** "float" ambient track, low volume. Float is the proven performer (11,796 avg plays across 3 uses, appears in 2 of top 3 breakouts). The chill vibe contrasts nicely with the bold opening claim.
- **Wardrobe:** Casual -- clean tee or light button-down. A Tudor or Omega on the wrist would be the perfect subtle flex (wearing the recommendation builds credibility).
- **Props Needed:** Green screen images: Tudor BB58 (black dial), Grand Seiko Snowflake SBGA211 (dial close-up AND full watch), Omega Speedmaster Moonwatch (hesalite classic), NASA moon photo. Optional: Rolex Submariner for the hook visual.
- **Lighting:** Clean ring light or key light. Bright, even -- keep it simple.
- **Editing Notes:** This should be one of the fastest videos to produce. Single take is possible. Cuts between watch images keep it visually dynamic. Target 25 seconds max -- the data shows 18-30 seconds is the sweet spot for this type of contrarian content.
