## FILMING NOTES

- **Camera Setup:** iPhone on tripod, vertical (9:16), eye-level. Green screen setup. Need clean key for graphic overlays.
- **Location:** Home office with green screen. Clean background essential -- the graphics are the star.
- **Pacing:** Medium-fast. Each price reveal gets its own beat. Let the numbers breathe for 1-2 seconds on screen before moving to the next watch. The pacing should feel like a receipt unrolling -- each reveal builds on the last.
- **Music/Sound:** "float" ambient track, very low under voice. The voice and price graphics carry this one. Music is just texture.
- **Wardrobe:** Clean, dark solid shirt. No patterns -- you don't want to compete with the graphics. Watch on wrist (any of the three featured watches, ideally the Tudor for the entry-luxury positioning).
- **Props Needed:** Pre-made price comparison graphics for all three watches. Green screen images: Datejust 41 (blue dial), Speedmaster Moonwatch (hesalite), Tudor BB58 (black dial). Each graphic should be clean, bold font, easy to read on mobile.
- **Lighting:** Even front lighting. Slightly cooler color temp to match the "data/transparency" vibe of the content.
- **Graphics Prep:** Create three simple price comparison cards before filming. White background, bold black text, green for savings. Keep them dead simple -- the viewer needs to absorb the numbers in 2-3 seconds.
