## FULL SCRIPT (Using Hook A)

### [0:00-0:03] HOOK
**Say:** "Your tax refund is sitting in your checking account doing absolutely nothing."
**Visual:** Direct to camera, green screen behind showing image of a bank app deposit notification or IRS refund check. Close-up framing, chest up.
**Text Overlay:** "YOUR REFUND THO"
**Energy:** Punchy, matter-of-fact, slight head shake like you're disappointed in them

### [0:03-0:08] SETUP
**Say:** "Average refund this year? About three grand. You know what three grand gets you on the grey market right now?"
**Visual:** Green screen switches to a clean graphic showing "$3,167 — avg. US tax refund 2026." Camera stays on face, tight framing.
**Text Overlay:** "$3,167 AVG REFUND"
**Energy:** Lean in slightly, building anticipation

### [0:08-0:14] WATCH 1 — TUDOR
**Say:** "A Tudor Black Bay 58. Retail's forty-one hundred. Grey market? Thirty-two hundred. You're saving nine hundred dollars and you're getting one of the best dive watches ever made."
**Visual:** Green screen shows Tudor BB58 — hero wrist shot, navy blue dial version. Cut to a second image showing the bracelet detail.
**Text Overlay:** "TUDOR BB58 — $3,200 GREY" then flash "RETAIL: $4,100" with strikethrough
**Energy:** Excited but controlled, like you're letting them in on something good

### [0:14-0:20] WATCH 2 — GRAND SEIKO
**Say:** "Grand Seiko Cocktail Time. Twenty-eight hundred on grey. The finishing on this thing embarrasses watches at triple the price. And I mean embarrasses."
**Visual:** Green screen shows Grand Seiko Cocktail Time dial macro — the textured dial catching light. Then a full wrist shot.
**Text Overlay:** "GRAND SEIKO — $2,800 GREY" then "FINISHING > WATCHES 3x THE PRICE"
**Energy:** Slow down slightly on "embarrasses" — let the word land, nod for emphasis

### [0:20-0:25] WATCH 3 — LONGINES
**Say:** "Longines Spirit. Fifteen hundred. CHRSC-certified chronometer, thirty-year heritage — this is a sleeper pick that nobody's talking about."
**Visual:** Green screen shows Longines Spirit on wrist — clean, tool watch aesthetic. Second image: caseback showing COSC certification.
**Text Overlay:** "LONGINES SPIRIT — $1,500 GREY" then "SLEEPER PICK"
**Energy:** Speed up slightly here, rapid-fire delivery, genuine enthusiasm

### [0:25-0:30] CTA
**Say:** "Your tax refund can sit in a savings account earning four percent, or it can sit on your wrist. DM me and I'll source whichever one you want."
**Visual:** Green screen shows all three watches side by side — clean comparison layout. Then cut to direct-to-camera, close up, slight smile.
**Text Overlay:** "DM FOR PRICING" with arrow pointing to profile
**Energy:** Confident close, direct eye contact, casual nod at the end

## FILMING NOTES

- **Camera Setup:** iPhone on tripod, vertical (9:16), chest-up framing. Green screen behind (solid green or use TikTok green screen effect with images loaded).
- **Location:** Home office or clean background area. Green screen effect handles the visuals.
- **Pacing:** Fast but not rushed. Each watch gets ~5-6 seconds. No dead air. Cut between watch images quickly — viewer should feel like they're flipping through options.
- **Music/Sound:** Use "float" or similar ambient/downtempo track. Keep it low under the voice — the commentary is the audio focus. Float averages 11,796 plays across 3 uses on this account.
- **Wardrobe:** Clean, casual luxury — plain dark tee or button-down with sleeves rolled. A watch on wrist (preferably one of the three mentioned).
- **Props Needed:** Phone with watch images loaded for green screen. If available: physical Tudor BB58, Grand Seiko Cocktail Time, or Longines Spirit for quick wrist flash at the end.
- **Lighting:** Ring light or natural window light, slightly warm. Nothing overproduced.
- **Editing Notes:** Quick cuts between watch images. Price text overlays should animate in (pop or slide). Strikethrough on retail price is a key visual moment — time it with the voice.
