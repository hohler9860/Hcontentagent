## FULL SCRIPT (Using Hook A)

**Text Overlay:** "Sub retail: $10,250" at 0:05, then "Grey: $12-13K" at 0:07, then "+20% PREMIUM" in red at 0:10

## FILMING NOTES

- **Camera Setup:** iPhone on tripod, vertical (9:16), eye-level. Green screen required -- the side-by-side visuals are essential.
- **Location:** Home office with green screen.
- **Pacing:** Medium pace with deliberate pauses. This is a debate format, not a rapid countdown. Let each price point land before moving to the next. The contrast is the content -- give the audience time to process "premium" vs "discount." Two beats of silence after "let that sit for a second" before continuing.
- **Music/Sound:** "float" ambient track, low volume. The ambient vibe creates tension without distraction. The voice carries the drama here.
- **Wardrobe:** Dark, clean. Either a Seamaster or Submariner on wrist -- ideally the Seamaster since that's the "winner" of the argument. Wearing your recommendation is credibility.
- **Props Needed:** Green screen images: Rolex Submariner (current ref, standard no-date or date), Omega Seamaster 300 (blue or black dial). Price comparison graphics: Submariner pricing with red "premium" indicator, Seamaster pricing with green "discount" indicator. Side-by-side comparison graphic for the verdict section.
- **Lighting:** Even, clean front lighting. The graphics need to be readable -- avoid shadows on the green screen that could interfere with the key.
- **Editing Notes:** The color contrast between red (Rolex premium) and green (Omega discount) is doing heavy lifting visually. Make sure the graphics are bold, high-contrast, and readable on a phone screen. Consider a subtle sound effect (cash register ding or record scratch) when the Omega discount is revealed to punctuate the contrast.
