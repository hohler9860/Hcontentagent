## FULL SCRIPT (Using Hook A)

### [0:00-0:03] HOOK

**Energy:** Immediate. Confident. Slight head shake like you can't believe this is even a debate.

**Visual:** Green screen -- photo of actual Costco Rolex display, then cut to photo of a Rolex Submariner. Creator gesturing casually.

**Say:** "Now go to your AD. You want a Submariner? Cool. First spend thirty to fifty thousand on jewelry your girl doesn't even want. Buy three Datejusts you'll flip at a loss. Wait twelve months. **Visual:** Green screen -- cycle through: stock photo of jewelry display, stack of Datejust boxes, calendar/clock graphic. Quick cuts, 2-3 seconds each. Creator's expression shifts from disbelief to frustration to amusement.
**Text Overlay:** "$30K-$50K in purchases" at 0:12 -- "$10K watch" at 0:17 -- "the math ain't mathing" at 0:19

### [0:27-0:32] CTA

## FILMING NOTES

- **Camera Setup:** iPhone on tripod, vertical (9:16), eye-level. Green screen behind creator (solid green sheet or portable green screen).
- **Location:** Home office or clean, neutral space. Just needs green screen wall.
- **Pacing:** Fast and punchy. No dead air. Every pause is intentional -- max 0.5 seconds between thoughts. Cut any "um" or hesitation in edit.
- **Music/Sound:** "float" or similar ambient/downtempo track, low volume under voice. Float averages 11,796 plays across 3 uses -- proven performer.
- **Wardrobe:** Clean casual -- solid color tee or button-down. No logos. A watch on the wrist (ideally a Submariner or similar Rolex to add subtle credibility).
- **Props Needed:** Green screen images preloaded: Costco storefront, Costco Rolex case, Rolex Submariner hero shot, jewelry display, Datejust boxes, price comparison graphic.
- **Lighting:** Ring light or key light from front, slightly above eye level. Even, clean lighting -- no dramatic shadows. This is commentary, not cinematic.
